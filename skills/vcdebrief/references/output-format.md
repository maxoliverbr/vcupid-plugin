# Meeting Debrief — Output Template

Use this exact structure when writing `vcdebrief-<fund>.md`:

```markdown
# Meeting Debrief: [Startup Name] × [Fund Name]
_Partner: [Partner Name] | Date: [meeting date] | Generated: [date]_

---

## Meeting Summary
- **Attendees:** [names and titles of everyone in the room]
- **Duration:** [approximate length]
- **Format:** [in-person / video / phone]

---

## Signal Read

### 🟢 Green Signals — Interest Indicators
- [Specific moment or question that showed genuine engagement]
- [e.g., "Asked for the data room link unprompted"]

### 🟡 Yellow Signals — Surface-Level or Hesitant
- [e.g., "Polite follow-ups on market size but didn't push further"]

### 🔴 Red Signals — Pushback or Misalignment
- [e.g., "Said 'we've seen a few of these' — didn't elaborate"]

_If no signals in a category: "None observed."_

---

## Diligence Prediction vs. Reality

| Question | Predicted by vcmatch? | Came Up? | Notes |
|----------|-----------------------|----------|-------|
| [question] | Yes / No | Yes / No | [how it was asked, what you said, how they reacted] |

**Unpredicted questions raised:**
- [Any question the vcmatch didn't anticipate]

---

## Open Diligence Queue

Items raised in the meeting that need a follow-up response:

| # | Question or Item Raised | Draft Answer / Material to Send | Owner | Deadline |
|---|------------------------|----------------------------------|-------|----------|
| 1 | [question] | [draft answer or "send [document]"] | [founder name] | [date] |

_If none: "No open items — all questions were addressed in the meeting."_

---

## Pitch Landing Assessment

**What landed:**
- [Specific element of the pitch that got a positive reaction — reference the vcmatch angle]

**What missed:**
- [Specific element that got pushback or low engagement]

**vcmatch prediction accuracy:** [Did the opening hook and pitch angle from the vcmatch report resonate as predicted? Brief honest assessment.]

---

## Follow-Up Email

```
Subject: [subject line — ≤8 words, specific to the meeting]

[4–6 sentence body]

[Founder Name]
[Title], [Company]
[Website]
```

_Send within 24 hours of the meeting._

---

## Recommended Next Step

**→ [Choose one: Request Second Meeting / Send Deck / Send Data Room / Give Space (follow up [date]) / Drop Fund]**

[1–2 sentences explaining why this is the right move based on the signals read]

---

## Updated Fit Signal

**Pre-meeting vcmatch score:** [X]/100 — [Recommended Action]
**Post-meeting read:** [Higher / Same / Lower]
**Why:** [1–2 sentences — what the meeting revealed that confirms or changes the vcmatch assessment]
```
